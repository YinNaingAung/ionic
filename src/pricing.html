Have a question?

What is the Community plan and who is it for?

The Community plan is a deeply discounted subscription for Ionic community members who are using Ionic for personal use, or as part of a very small business or early stage startup with less than USD $500,000 in annual revenue or total funds raised. This includes students, hobbyists, entrepreneurs, and professional developers working on side projects. The Community plan may not be used by businesses with more than USD $500,000 in annual revenue. For more information, check out our dedicated Community Plan FAQ page.

Which plans include access to native solutions like Identity Vault, Auth Connect, and Secure Storage?

The Basic plan does not include access to these solutions. The Standard plan provides access to Identity Vault, Auth Connect, and Secure Storage only, and is limited to one app per solution. If you would like to purchase additional licenses for more than one app, contact our Sales team to customize your plan. The Enterprise plan includes access to all of our native solutions, including Payments, and premier integrations, such as Microsoft Intune, Couchbase, and more. If you would like access to these solutions, contact our team about purchasing an Enterprise plan. Or, build your own custom plan with our custom plan builder.

How do Live Update limits scale?

Appflow allows you to push hot code updates directly to your users’ devices for any modifications to your web code. A single Live Update is one code update to one device. If you send one update to 200 devices, that counts as 200 Live Updates. If you send two updates to 200 devices, that counts as 400 Live Updates. For most customers, Live Update quantity isn't an issue. If you believe that you might need a very large quantity of updates please reach out to our Sales team to discuss the best way to scale your growth. Basic and Standard plans start at 25,000 Live Updates per month. Any further increase must be a custom package as we also take into account the size (and "shape") of your updates.

How can I use Appflow with my own CI/CD Tool?

For teams and businesses that wish to integrate Appflow with their existing CI/CD platform, the Standard and Enterprise plans include access to the Cloud CLI to kick off builds and deployments as steps in your own CI/CD tool. This allows you to use the powerful CI/CD tooling you already use, while offloading the complexity of mobile DevOps on to Appflow. The Cloud CLI can be installed in any CI/CD tool like Travis, Jenkins, CircleCI, Azure DevOps, Gitlab CI, BitBucket Server, etc.
